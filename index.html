Frequently asked questions

What is AlgoHive? ▾

A platform to discover, track, and allocate to managed strategies — with performance fees, not AUM fees.

How do fees work? ▾

We focus on success-based fees aligned to returns. Plans cover tools, data, and premium features.

Can I track multiple strategies? ▾

Yes — Freemium tracks one. Basic tracks up to six. Premium unlocks unlimited tracking.

Do you offer advisory or just tools? ▾

The platform provides tools, data, and optional bookable sessions. It’s not financial advice.

Where is my data stored? ▾

Encrypted at rest and in transit with reputable cloud providers. You control connections and revocations.

Can I cancel anytime? ▾

Yep. Plans are month-to-month. You can downgrade or cancel from your account settings.
