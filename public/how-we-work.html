How It Works

Your journey to a stronger portfolio starts here.

We've streamlined the process of discovering and allocating to managed strategies. Follow four simple steps to take control of your financial future.

1

Discover Strategies

Explore our curated list of "OpenStrategies" across different asset classes like Equities, FX, and Crypto. Each strategy is backed by historical data and a clear methodology, allowing you to find a fit for your risk appetite and financial goals.

2

Track & Analyze

Use our free Silver plan to track the performance of one strategy in real-time. When you're ready for more, upgrade to our Gold plan to monitor up to six strategies and access premium wealth-tracking tools and insights.

3

Allocate & Grow

Ready to invest? Allocate funds to your chosen strategies. You only pay a performance fee on your returns, never on your assets under management. This is our commitment to a partnership where we only succeed when you do.

4

Leverage Your Assets

Unlock the value of your portfolio without selling your holdings. Our portfolio-backed lending allows you to access flexible, low-cost credit, helping you meet liquidity needs while your investments continue to grow.
