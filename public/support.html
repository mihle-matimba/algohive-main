Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by going to the login page and clicking "Forgot Password". You will receive an email with instructions to create a new one.
How do I set up my first strategy?
Navigate to the "My Strategies" section from your portfolio, click on "Create New Strategy", and follow the on-screen wizard to configure your parameters, risk management, and linked account.
Which payment methods are accepted?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, and direct bank transfers. All options are available on the funding page.

Troubleshooting

Why is my bot not executing trades?
This can be due to several reasons: 1) Insufficient funds in your linked account. 2) The market is closed. 3) API key permissions are incorrect. 4) The strategy's conditions have not been met. Check your bot's log for specific error messages.
I'm getting connection errors.
Connection errors usually point to an issue with your API keys or your brokerage's server. Please double-check that your API keys are correct and have the necessary permissions for trading. You can also check our System Status page for any ongoing platform issues.
