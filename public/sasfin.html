Primary Menu

Left sidebar on desktop · Top drawer on mobile

1. Dashboard

Performance snapshot

Overview of mandate performance with quick stats, charts, exposure, and notifications.

  • AUM, YTD return, MTM PnL, risk score, allocation summary.
  • Equity curve, monthly returns heatmap, exposure by asset class.
  • Notifications for audits, mandate updates, and account actions.

2. Portfolio

Holdings & performance
  • Holdings: instruments list, weights, current price vs average cost, unrealized PnL.
  • Allocation: model vs actual with drift alerts; rebalancing actions (view-only).
  • Performance: time-weighted return, drawdowns, volatility, benchmarks, contribution analysis.

3. Mandates

View-only

Inventory of mandates created for Sasfin, with risk levels, strategy mix, and objective summaries. Editable only by AlgoHive.

4. Strategies

Filtered catalog
  • List all AlgoHive strategies with filters for risk, asset class, region, and volatility band.
  • Detail pages show factsheet, performance chart, Sharpe/Sortino, max drawdown, win rate.
  • Surface capacity limits and historical allocation behaviour. Eligible strategies match the approved mandate.

5. Transactions

Ledger-ready
  • Deposits, withdrawals, trade activity from automated execution.
  • Management and performance fees surfaced alongside a clean ledger view.
  • Export to CSV/PDF with reconciliation-ready breakdowns.

6. Clients

If applicable
  • Client list with AUM, subscription status, and risk profile.
  • Portfolio status plus compliance/KYC state.
  • Restricted account actions suited for Sasfin’s operating model.

7. Compliance

Oversight
  • KYC status by client or fund, mandate sign-offs, and regulatory reports.
  • Transaction monitoring logs (lightweight) and risk flags.

8. Reports

Download center
  • Monthly reports, quarterly factsheets, tax certificates, performance summaries, statements.
  • Central download center with consistent formatting.

9. Settings

Control
  • Sasfin account profile, user roles & permissions (Admin, Analyst, Viewer).
  • API integrations (optional), email notifications, brand customization (logo & theme).

10. Support

Assurance
  • Dedicated AlgoHive institutional support chat and ticketing center.
  • Onboarding guide and SLA documentation to keep responses predictable.
